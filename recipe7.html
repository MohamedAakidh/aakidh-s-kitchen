Shakshuka

Poached eggs nestled in a spiced tomato and pepper sauce, finished with fresh herbs.
Hearty, vibrant, and perfect for breakfast or brunch!

⏳ Prep: 10 min 🍳 Cook: 25 min 🍽️ Servings: 3 🔥 Calories: 210
🧂Ingredients
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper (optional, for heat)
  • 1 can (400g) chopped tomatoes
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 4–5 large eggs
  • Fresh cilantro or parsley, for garnish
  • To serve:
    • Crusty bread or pita
👨‍🍳Instructions
  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and bell pepper and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Stir in garlic, cumin, paprika, and cayenne. Cook for 1 minute, until fragrant.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes, season with salt and pepper, and simmer for 10–12 minutes, until the sauce thickens slightly.
  4. Make small wells in the sauce and gently crack eggs into each well.
  5. Cover the skillet and cook until eggs are just set, 6–8 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro or parsley. Serve hot with crusty bread or pita.
