Chicken Curry

This aromatic chicken curry blends rich spices with tender chicken...

Ingredients

  • Chicken (cut into pieces)
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Curry powder
  • Coconut milk
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Sauté onions, garlic, and ginger in oil.
  2. Add chicken and cook until sealed.
  3. Stir in curry powder and tomatoes.
  4. Pour in coconut milk and simmer for 25 minutes.
  5. Season to taste and serve with rice or roti.
