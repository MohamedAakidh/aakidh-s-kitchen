Fresh Summer Salad

Crisp, colorful veggies tossed together with a zesty lemon-honey vinaigrette.
Vibrant, refreshing, and perfect for sunny days!

‚è≥ Prep: 15 min üç≥ Cook: 30 min üçΩÔ∏è Servings: 2-4 üî• Calories: 120
üßÇIngredients
  • 1 large cucumber, diced
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup sweet corn (canned or fresh)
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled (optional)
  • For the dressing:
    • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
    • 1 tbsp lemon juice
    • 1 tsp honey
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 1/4 tsp black pepper
üë®‚Äçüç≥Instructions
  1. In a large salad bowl, combine cucumber, tomatoes, bell pepper, red onion, sweet corn, and parsley.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, honey, salt, and pepper until well blended.
  3. Drizzle the dressing over the vegetables and toss gently to coat everything evenly.
  4. Add crumbled feta cheese on top, if using.
  5. Chill the salad for 10 minutes if you like, then serve immediately for the freshest flavor.
