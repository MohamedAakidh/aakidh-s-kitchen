Mango Lassi

Creamy, refreshing mango yogurt drink, sweetened and spiced for the perfect summer treat.
Tropical, cooling, and ready in minutes!

⏳ Prep: 5 min 🍳 Cook: 0 min 🍽️ Servings: 2 🔥 Calories: 160
🧂Ingredients
  • 1 cup ripe mango, peeled and chopped (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 cup plain yogurt (full-fat or low-fat)
  • 1/2 cup cold milk (or water for a lighter lassi)
  • 2–3 tbsp sugar or honey, to taste
  • 1/4 tsp ground cardamom
  • 4–5 ice cubes
  • Optional toppings:
    • Chopped pistachios
    • Mango pieces
    • Mint leaves
👨‍🍳Instructions
  1. Place mango, yogurt, milk, sugar (or honey), cardamom, and ice cubes in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth, creamy, and frothy.
  3. Taste and adjust sweetness or thickness as desired by adding more sugar or milk.
  4. Pour into glasses. Top with pistachios, mango pieces, or mint leaves if desired.
  5. Serve immediately, chilled.
