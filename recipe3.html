Ultimate Veggie Burger

Hearty, flavorful burger patties packed with vegetables and spices, served on a toasted bun with all your favorite fixings.
Satisfying, healthy, and 100% meat-free delight!

  • 1 can (400g) black beans, drained and rinsed

  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup corn kernels
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley or cilantro
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

  • 1/2 tsp salt

  • 2 tbsp olive oil (for cooking)
  • To serve:
    • 4 burger buns, toasted
    • Lettuce leaves
    • Tomato slices
    • Red onion rings
    • Cheese slices (optional)

  1. In a large bowl, mash the black beans with a fork or potato masher until mostly smooth but still a little chunky.
  2. Add grated carrot, breadcrumbs, chopped onion, corn, parsley, garlic, paprika, cumin, salt, pepper, and egg. Mix until well combined.
  3. With damp hands, form the mixture into 4 thick patties.
  4. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook patties for 4â€“5 minutes per side, until golden and firm.
  5. Toast the burger buns. Layer with lettuce, tomato, onion, cheese (if using), and sauces as desired.
  6. Place the veggie patties on buns, assemble your burgers, and enjoy hot!
