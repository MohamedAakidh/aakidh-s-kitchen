Chocolate Avocado Mousse

Creamy, rich chocolate mousse made with ripe avocados and natural sweeteners.
Decadent, healthy, and ready in minutes!

⏳ Prep: 10 min 🍳 Cook: 0 min 🍽️ Servings: 4 🔥 Calories: 220
🧂Ingredients
  • 2 ripe avocados, peeled and pitted
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup milk of choice (dairy or plant-based)
  • 1/4 cup honey or maple syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Optional toppings:
    • Fresh berries
    • Shaved dark chocolate
    • Chopped nuts
    • Whipped cream
👨‍🍳Instructions
  1. Add avocado, cocoa powder, milk, honey (or maple syrup), vanilla, and a pinch of salt to a food processor or blender.
  2. Blend until completely smooth and creamy, scraping down the sides as needed.
  3. Taste and adjust sweetness or chocolate flavor if desired.
  4. Spoon mousse into serving cups. Chill for at least 30 minutes for best texture.
  5. Before serving, top with berries, chocolate shavings, nuts, or whipped cream as desired.
  6. Enjoy your healthy, chocolatey dessert!
