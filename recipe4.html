Classic Margherita Pizza

Thin, crispy crust topped with tangy tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella, and fragrant basil leaves.
Simple, timeless, and bursting with authentic flavor!

⏳ Prep: 20 min 🍳 Cook: 15 min 🍽️ Servings: 2 🔥 Calories: 400
🧂Ingredients
  • 1 pizza base (store-bought or homemade, ~12-inch)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce (preferably pizza sauce or passata)
  • 150g fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced or torn
  • 8-10 fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Optional:
    • Pinch of dried oregano
    • Grated parmesan cheese
👨‍🍳Instructions
  1. Preheat your oven to 250°C (480°F), or as hot as it will go. Place a pizza stone or baking tray inside to heat.
  2. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the pizza base, leaving a small border at the edges.
  3. Arrange the mozzarella slices evenly over the sauce. Season lightly with salt and pepper, and sprinkle with a pinch of oregano if desired.
  4. Bake on the preheated stone or tray for 10–15 minutes, until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling and lightly browned.
  5. Remove from the oven. Top with fresh basil leaves and drizzle with olive oil.
  6. Slice and serve immediately. Enjoy your classic Margherita pizza!
