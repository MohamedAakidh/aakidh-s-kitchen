Honey Garlic Chicken

Juicy chicken pieces glazed in a sticky, sweet and garlicky sauce.
Quick, flavorful, and perfect for any night!

⏳ Prep: 10 min 🍳 Cook: 20 min 🍽️ Servings: 4 🔥 Calories: 370
🧂Ingredients
  • 600g boneless chicken thighs or breasts, cut into chunks
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar (or apple cider vinegar)
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds, for garnish
  • 2 spring onions, sliced, for garnish
  • To serve:
    • Steamed rice
    • Steamed broccoli or veggies
👨‍🍳Instructions
  1. Season chicken pieces with salt and pepper, then toss with cornstarch until evenly coated.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook until golden and cooked through, about 6–8 minutes. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add minced garlic and sauté for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Stir in honey, soy sauce, vinegar, and water. Bring to a simmer.
  5. Return chicken to the pan, toss to coat, and cook for 2–3 minutes until sauce thickens and glazes the chicken.
  6. Garnish with sesame seeds and spring onions. Serve hot over steamed rice with veggies.
